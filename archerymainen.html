Archery as a hobby

Archery is a sport, which offers great equipments and tremendous excitement. Archery offers a great counterbalance to the busy rhythm of life, as the archery enthusiast is forced to learn to focus on only relevant, skill and nerves under control. Archery is a modern sport that suits everyone. Contrary to popular belief archery is not the power sport, but a demanding technique species to which the enthusiast must manage themselves in every situation.

If you are interested in starting archery hobby and you want to really develop to a good archer, you'll want to join the nearest archery club. Archery hobby alone will not take just the beginning step further. Finland has a nearly sixty followed across the country, all of which give you guidance.

Archery is a great deal of time and perseverance demanding precision sport in which you do not evolve into the champion instantly. To hit every arrow in the middle of tens, you need to learn the proper shooting technique and the dogged pursuit of practice to learn to manage yourself and your equipment.

Archery hobby is at the best, a good workout with nice people, competition of superiority of the club and national level, or diamond-hard struggle for victory in the international arena. Archery is a challenging but also rewarding hobby.

Variety of bow types, age ranges and forms of competition are numerous. In most archery clubs in Finland, you can practice target archery or field archery. The most popular bow types are a familiar sight Olympics bow, both technically slightly more complicated compound bowa. In addition, you can enjoy archery with a traditional bow, or long bow, which belongs to the so-called instinctive shooting without a sight.

The youngest of archery enthusiasts are 10-12 years old mini-juniors, while the senior archers can be over seventy years old. Archery should not start too early, however, because in less than 10 years of age, muscles and bones have not yet developed for such a demanding sport, neither do they possess patience for a long-term training.
